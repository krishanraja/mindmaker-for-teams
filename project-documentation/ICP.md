# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Primary User: Workshop Facilitator

**Who they are:**
- Independent consultants or internal transformation leads
- Running 3-hour executive alignment workshops
- Comfortable with live facilitation and AI concepts
- Need real-time data capture during sessions

**What they need:**
- QR code generation for rapid mobile submission
- Live aggregation of participant inputs (bottlenecks, votes, reactions)
- AI-powered clustering and synthesis during breaks
- Professional output (decision framework report)
- Reliable tech that doesn't fail mid-workshop

**What they don't need:**
- Complex configuration or pre-workshop setup
- Training on how to facilitate (they already know)
- Dashboard analytics or longitudinal tracking
- Client management or billing features

## Secondary User: Workshop Participant (Executive)

**Who they are:**
- C-suite or senior leadership team members (CEO, CFO, COO, CTO, etc.)
- Responsible for AI transformation decisions
- Time-constrained (3 hours max)
- Skeptical of surveys and assessments

**What they need:**
- Mobile-first submission (scan QR, submit, done)
- No login, no accounts, no friction
- Fast input methods (not essays, quick selections)
- Credible output that reflects actual discussion

**What they don't need:**
- Personal dashboards or accounts
- Email reports or follow-up surveys
- Training or onboarding
- Long-term engagement

## Company Profile

**Ideal company characteristics:**
- 50-5000 employees (mid-market to enterprise)
- Considering or starting AI transformation
- Has executive team that meets regularly
- Can allocate 3 hours for team alignment
- Willing to test decisions through simulation

**Industries:**
- Professional services (consulting, legal, finance)
- Technology companies (SaaS, platforms)
- Healthcare organizations
- Manufacturing and logistics
- Government and public sector

## Anti-ICP

**This is NOT for:**
- Individual leaders (requires executive team)
- Companies wanting ongoing assessments or tracking
- Teams needing multi-day workshops or training programs
- Organizations requiring deep integration with HR/learning systems
- Startups with <5 person leadership teams

## Acquisition Channels

- Direct outreach to facilitators/consultants
- Word-of-mouth from workshop participants
- LinkedIn presence showing workshop outputs
- Referrals from companies that completed workshops

## Pricing Expectations

- Facilitators expect per-workshop pricing or monthly subscription
- Companies expect workshop cost to be <$5k for 3-hour session
- Volume discounts for multiple workshops
- No expectation of free tier (professional tool)
